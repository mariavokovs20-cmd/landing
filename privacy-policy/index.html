Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

GSC respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website, request an estimate, submit a form, call us, or otherwise communicate with us about remodeling services.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you choose to provide, including your name, phone number, email address, project address, preferred appointment times, remodeling goals, budget range, photos or notes you submit, and any other details you include in a message or estimate request.

We may also collect basic website usage information, such as pages viewed, referring pages, device type, browser type, IP address, and general interaction data. This information helps us maintain and improve the website.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to respond to inquiries, schedule consultations, prepare estimates, provide remodeling services, send project-related communications, improve our website, maintain business records, and comply with legal obligations.

Calls, Texts, and Emails

By submitting your contact information, you authorize GSC to contact you about your inquiry by phone, text message, or email. Message and data rates may apply. You may ask us to stop contacting you at any time.

Sharing Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers, subcontractors, scheduling tools, marketing or website vendors, payment processors, professional advisors, or authorities when reasonably necessary to operate our business, fulfill your request, or comply with the law.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies, analytics tools, and similar technologies to understand website traffic and improve performance. You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies, though some site features may not work as intended.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect information. No website, email, or electronic storage system can be guaranteed completely secure.

Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to respond to your request, provide services, maintain business records, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and meet legal or accounting requirements.

Your Choices

You may request that we update, correct, or delete personal information we maintain about you, subject to legal and business recordkeeping requirements. You may also opt out of promotional communications.

Children's Privacy

Our website and services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised update date.

Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to GSC through the contact form or phone number listed on our website.
